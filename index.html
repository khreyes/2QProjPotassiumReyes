The website will be about the Olympic class-ocean liners, a trio of ships built in the 1900s. In this website, it will showcase their innovations, history, and a lot of interesting things about them. The content pages will include one page discussing the Olympic class, then one page for each ship, including its technical attributes, its history, and its significance.
